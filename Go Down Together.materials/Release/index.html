A short game made for an album discussion. Two friends reminisce in their home before they experience the end of the world.

Go Down Together was created with Inform and has IFID 0ED463DB-1667-41DE-8DA1-63776DEA37A9. To play a work like this one, you need an interpreter program: many are available, among them Zoom for Mac OS X and for Unix; Windows Frotz or Windows Glulxe for Windows. Or you can play without downloading anything by following the 'Play In-Browser' link, using the Quixe interpreter. You'll need to have Javascript enabled on your web browser.
